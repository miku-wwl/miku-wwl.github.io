My current learning and practice focus is platform engineering: Terraform, Docker, Kubernetes, Kustomize, GitHub Actions, CI/CD, Argo CD, Argo Rollouts, canary deployments, observability, monitoring, and reliable cloud infrastructure.

This builds on my backend experience at Huawei and OKX. At Huawei, I worked on Spring Boot microservices deployed on Kubernetes and investigated defects with logs, tracing, packet analysis, debugging tools, and production-oriented troubleshooting workflows. At OKX, I gained exposure to event-driven architecture and reliability-sensitive trading infrastructure.

The direction I care about is practical: reusable modules, testable automation, clear deployment workflows, strong feedback loops, and infrastructure that helps engineering teams move faster without losing reliability.
